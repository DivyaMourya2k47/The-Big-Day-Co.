Our Story

Founded in 2010 by visionary event planner Isabella Martinez, The Big Day Co. began as a dream to transform ordinary moments into extraordinary memories. What started as a small boutique planning service has evolved into one of the most prestigious event planning companies in the industry.

Our journey began when Isabella, after years of working in luxury hospitality, recognized the need for truly personalized, high-end event planning services. She assembled a team of creative professionals who shared her passion for perfection and attention to detail.

Today, we've planned over 1,500 events, from intimate gatherings to grand celebrations, each one reflecting our commitment to excellence and our clients' unique visions. Our reputation for creating magical moments has made us the preferred choice for discerning clients who demand nothing but the best.
